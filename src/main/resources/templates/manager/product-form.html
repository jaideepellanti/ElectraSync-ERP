ℹ️ How stock works in ElectraSync:
Every new product starts at 0 stock. There is no way to manually type in a stock number — this is intentional. To bring stock into the store, go to Purchase Orders → New, select this product and a vendor, enter the quantity and price you're paying, then mark the order as Received. This keeps a full record of exactly which vendor supplied every unit and what you paid for it. Stock decreases automatically when a sale is made.
